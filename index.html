Tony Ferguson

American Professional Mixed Martial Artist
  • Age: 34
  • Hometown: Oxnard, California, U.S.

Anthony Armand "Tony" Ferguson Padilla (born February 12, 1984) is an American professional mixed martial artist currently competing in the lightweight division of the Ultimate Fighting Championship (UFC). A professional competitor since 2008, Ferguson was the winner of The Ultimate Fighter 13. In 2017, Ferguson defeated Kevin Lee to become the interim UFC Lightweight Champion

After winning the interim UFC Lightweight Championship in 2017, he set a new record for most consecutive wins in UFC lightweight division history (with 10) and became widely regarded by pundits as one of the best lightweights in the history of the UFC. As of August 8, 2018, he is #2 in the official UFC lightweight rankings and he is #13 in official UFC pound-for-pound rankings.
